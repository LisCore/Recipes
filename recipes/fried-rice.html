Fried Rice

Description

Recipe for Fried Rice!

Ingredients

  • Day old rice
  • MSG
  • Chinese sausage or your choice of meat
  • Carrots/Peas packaged
  • Fried Rice Powder packet
  • Eggs
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. If using different meats, cook them
  2. In seperate panCook garlic/frozen veges
  3. cook eggs with the pan with veges
  4. Add rice
  5. Add sauce/powder
  6. Place in meats
  7. Enjoy!
