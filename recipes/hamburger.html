Hamburger

Description

This is a hamburger recipe!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (80 lean, 20 fat)
  • Potato buns
  • Your pick of cheese
  • Pickles
  • Onions
  • Sauce of your choice
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Cook patty
  2. Place cheese so that it melts
  3. Place buns on grill
  4. Assemble burger
  5. Place lettuce, onion, pickles on patty
  6. Administer sauce on top bun
  7. Place patty on bottom bun
  8. Enjoy!
