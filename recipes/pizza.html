Pizza

Description

This is a pizza recipe!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Spread out dough
  2. Scoop one cup of tomato sauce onto dough
  3. Use circling motion to spread out sauce evenly
  4. Sprinkle one cup of cheese onto sauce
  5. Place good amount of Pepperoni
  6. Place dough/pizza on oven mat
  7. Cook for 30 minutes on 350 degrees F
  8. Enjoy!
